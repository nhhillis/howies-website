Until a week ago, I knew little to nothing about building a website. Now you are looking at my first gaudy website! Tacky color scheme and all!

I am a biologist by training, but my interests are varied and wide-ranging. I enjoy exploring the photography, art, science and technology, and I'm always excited to learn something new. This website is a result of that curiosity.

I like to think of myself as a creative person. My creative passions include photography and print making, but they branch out into painting and now web development. I never looked at coding or programming or web development as a creative pursuit, but now I see it as a form of art and expression. The creative satisfaction of building a website is something I never expected to feel.

This website will be my little corner of the internet — a place to dump my photos, share my projects, and ramble about whatever has caught my attention lately. Science, technology, culture, art — if it interests me, it'll probably end up here."

I am not sure if this is a manifesto or the ramblings of a curious mind. I hope whoever reads this finds inspiration or at least something to think about

Let this be a reminder to follow your curiosity and keep exploring!

- Howie
