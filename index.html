About Me

My name is Howie, and I am a biologist by day and a multipotentialite by night. I am drawn to making things—photographs, prints, paintings, bits of code, and the occasional garden bed. I am equally at home wandering backroads with a camera as I am experimenting with inks, building small tools, or tending plants. This website is my place to gather all those threads: the projects, the curiosities, the half-formed ideas, and the finished pieces. I hope exploring it feels a little like stepping into my workshop—full of variety, a bit eclectic, and always made with genuine curiosity.
